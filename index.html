01 — About Me

The Editor
Behind the Frame

I'm Amar Enp, a professional Video Editor and Digital Content Creator born on January 28, 2001, in Monufia Governorate, Egypt. I turned a passion for visual storytelling into a full-time craft — cutting footage is not just my job, it's the language I'm most fluent in.

Having completed both my formal education and military service, I bring a rare combination of academic discipline and real-world resilience to every project. No obligations stand between me and a serious professional commitment — I'm fully available and ready to deliver.

The same discipline that defines my daily routine is what I bring to every timeline: precision, consistency, and an uncompromising standard of delivery. I don't just finish projects. I finish them right.
